Rainbow Six Operator Playstyles

Click dot to see info about that player.
This visualization utilizes principal component analysis to put the 40-dimensional data of operator playtimes onto two dimensions. The x-direction shows a player's bias towards Twitch, Ash, Bandit, and Blackbeard and against Thermite and Thatcher. The y-direction shows a player's bias towards Ash, Thermite, Valk, and Capitao, and against Nomad, Vigil, and Twitch.
The coloring shows groupings of similar players according to k-means clustering.
